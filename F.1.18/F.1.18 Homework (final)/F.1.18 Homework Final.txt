You need to create a website according to the template on your own. 
The website template is in the folder. The name of the folder is Homework (final). 
In the folder there are the template and the design document (sizes, colors). Also, there are images in the img folder, in the png folder there are images for the cards and background, and in the icon folder there are icons for the cards of the 3rd section and the logo of the website for the menu.
